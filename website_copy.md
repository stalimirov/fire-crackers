### Blaze (`/the-crew/blaze`)

**Headline:** BLAZE
**Sub-headline:** Chili Pepper / De Facto Leader

**Bio:**
Nobody elected Blaze. Nobody had to. He walked into Shipka's factory one afternoon with a recipe and an opinion, and by the time anyone thought to ask where he came from, he was already in charge.

He's overconfident, occasionally catastrophic, and almost always right about the things that matter. The wick on his head burns brighter when he's excited — which is most of the time. The grin hasn't changed once.

Blaze is the first Fire Cracker you'll meet. He will absolutely take credit for that.

## The Factory Page (`/the-factory`)

### Opening
**Full-viewport headline:** Against All Blandness.

### Chapter 1: THE FACTORY
Somewhere in Bulgaria, in a building that's been making things longer than anyone remembers, the crackers get made.

It's not clean. It's not optimized. The oven has a name. The walls have opinions. Shipka has been running it since before the crew arrived, and he'll be running it long after any CFA citation attempts to say otherwise.

This is where it starts.

### Chapter 2: THE CREW
They came from different places. None of them were looking for this. Blaze showed up first, Matchstick close behind — the others arrived the way things tend to in a good story: exactly when they needed to.

The Corporate Flavor Authority has been filing forms about this factory for two years. Mr. Bland has visited twice. The crackers keep getting made.

Nobody asked them to. That's the point.

### Chapter 3: THE INGREDIENTS
Few ingredients. Real ones. Sourced from Bulgarian farms, markets, and suppliers who know what they're growing. The chili comes from a family operation outside Plovdiv. The chickpeas are from the Thracian plain. The ghost pepper is a project Shipka started three years ago in the factory yard.

It says "Made in Bulgaria" on the pack because it is made in Bulgaria, by people who know what that means.

**Ingredients grid caption:** From Bulgarian fields.

### The Values
**BOLD** — No half measures. If it's worth making, it's worth committing to.
**CRAFT** — Few ingredients. Real sourcing. The opposite of anonymous factory food.
**IRREVERENT** — The 18+ mark is a statement, not a disclaimer.
**AUTHENTIC** — Bulgarian roots. Global aesthetic. No pretending.
**CHARACTER** — The brand has a cast, a world, a story. The snack is the beginning of it.
