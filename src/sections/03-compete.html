Global benchmarks — what the category can become

🇺🇸 United States
Pirate's Booty
Character-driven snack brand (Captain Bob + Crunchy the parrot). Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade float. Acquired by Hershey Company. The IP was the acquisition value.
→ Brands with mascots have 37% higher chance of increasing market share
🇰🇷 South Korea
Samyang Buldak + Hochi
Chicken mascot with full personality depth (bad fashion sense, addicted to spice). Started as one ramen SKU — now 32+ products. EU expansion 2024. Hochi justifies the heat: "even he struggles with 3× spicy."
→ Character IP → portfolio expansion → international distribution
🇯🇵 Japan
Matsuzaki Senbei (est. 1804)
8th-generation artisan senbei maker in Ginza. Hand-painted seasonal motifs. Individual crackers priced up to €20. Proof that craft crackers support genuinely premium pricing when the story is real.
→ Kaki-no-tane = the original bar snack. Every izakaya serves it.
🇮🇩 Indonesia
Maicih (since 2010)
Spicy cassava chips with a 10-level numbered heat scale. Single founder, cottage start. Scaled to 80+ distributors nationally. The numbered scale created a purchase ritual — consumers identify as "a Level 7 person."
→ Numbered heat levels drive repeat purchase aspiration
