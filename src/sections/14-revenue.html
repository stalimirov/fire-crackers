The honest verdict

This is a niche business, not a venture-scale one. It requires ~€3,500 to reach breakeven at Month 13–14. All founders work for equity — no salary overhead. The conservative model uses 20 packs/venue ramp-up month, then 40/month steady, and includes all operational overhead (accountant, delivery, insurance). Real performance could be significantly better — tigre tigre sold 1,000 jars quickly with less brand differentiation. Year 2–3 projections (€250K–500K revenue, 150+ venues) will be modeled after Month 6 data review, based on actual sell-through and co-packer quotes.
