Manufacturing the "Grid Snap"

To streamline production and keep it replicable in any conventional bakery kitchen, we bypass complex extrusion and opt for a Sheeted "Grid-Cut" Cracker. Dough is rolled to a 1/16" thickness, docked to ensure an even bake, and rotary-cut into flush squares. This yields a massive crunch with 0% dough-webbing waste.
