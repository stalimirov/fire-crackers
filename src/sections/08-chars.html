Blaze
Red Chili Pepper · SKU #2 · Heat Tier: Hot

De facto leader. Nobody voted for him. Nobody needed to — he was already doing the job. Blaze doesn't market crackers. He lives out the reason bland food is the enemy. When Mr. Bland files another complaint, Blaze is the one who reads it aloud and laughs.

  • SKU: Blaze's Original — the flagship, Tier 2 Hot
  • Visual DNA: Mid-century print mascot, bold outlines, halftone depth, tattoo-flash ready
  • Art style: Bold black outlines, flat fills, halftone dots, limited palette
  • Story role: Protagonist — Season 1 arc driver, factory founder
  • Competes with: Every brand that uses a stock photo of a pepper
Flat style treatment — single-colour contexts, merch, stamps
