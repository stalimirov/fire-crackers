04 — Our Advantage

Three moats, not one.

Any single pillar is copyable. All three together, in combination, on a 12-month timeline — are not.

🌶️
The Product
  • Crackers, not chips — different form factor, different occasion
  • Real Bulgarian ingredients, named sources
  • 5-flame heat hierarchy — every tier is a distinct SKU
  • Kraft pouch + wax seal — unboxing is part of the brand
  • LTO drops built in from Stage 2 — scarcity is structural
🎨
The Brand IP
  • 8 fully developed characters — not mascots, a cast
  • Animated mini-series — content engine, not just ads
  • Mid-century print mascot visual language — instantly recognisable
  • American Traditional tattoo DNA — community entry point
  • Character IP that compounds: merch, licensing, collabs
🍺
The Channel
  • Venue-first — bars, board game cafes, cocktail bars, specialty shops
  • Sale-or-return at launch — zero risk for venues
  • 100% venue markup — owners love it, no fridge, no prep
  • 700–900 addressable venues nationally, 350–500 in Sofia alone
  • Venue relationships are sticky — they don't switch on price

This is a brand business with crackers as the entry product —
not a cracker business that does branding.
