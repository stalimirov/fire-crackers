Bootstrappable — by design

No external investor needed for Stage 1. All founders work for equity — zero salary overhead. No HACCP consultant needed (kitchen is already certified). No co-packer until month 7–10 when revenue justifies it. Ongoing overhead: accountant (~€50/mo), delivery runs (~€30/mo), liability insurance (~€25/mo).

Legally required even as distributor: batch traceability log (1-page spreadsheet per batch — supplier, date, quantity, who received it) and a recall procedure document. Both are lightweight but non-negotiable under EU Reg 178/2002. Shared kitchen also requires an allergen cross-contamination note on the label if the facility handles common allergens.

Contract production model

We never own production risk

Fire Crackers registers as a brand owner and food distributor, not a food producer. Label reads: "Produced by [Kitchen/Co-packer] for Fire Crackers OOD."

This means: no HACCP plan creation, no facility inspections, no temperature monitoring logs, no supplier audit records. The certified kitchen (Stage 1) and co-packer (Stage 2) carry all production compliance.
