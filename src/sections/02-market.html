The craft beer parallel

Craft beer succeeded in Bulgaria using the same playbook Fire Crackers intends: premium adult product, bar-first distribution, irreverent branding, 200–400% venue markup, Instagram-native content. The consumer, the venue, and the price tolerance are all already there.

Why now
  • — Hot/spicy snacks globally proven; Eastern Europe 3–5 years behind
  • — Bar snack category is unclaimed in Bulgaria
  • — Consumers distrust anonymous factory food — local + few ingredients wins
  • — Premium segment growing 10.32% CAGR, 3× overall market
EU chilli demand is surging

European chilli pepper imports grew 33% in four years (48,500 → 64,000 tonnes, 2019–2023), driven by ethnic diversity (35M+ EU residents born outside the bloc) and rising consumer interest in world cuisines — Indian, Thai, Mexican, African. Bulgaria is the 6th-largest EU importer of chilli peppers (2,286 tonnes in 2023), signalling established local appetite. Romania next door saw 118% import growth in the same period.
