08 — Content Strategy

Content is not a support
function. It is the brand.

Static-first content powered by a co-founder designer, Live2D character rigs, and near-zero tool costs (~€10/mo). The animated mini-series is a Stage 2 deliverable — Stage 1 builds the visual universe through illustration, character reveals, and short animated clips.

Stage 1 — Content Pipeline
Static-First, Near-Zero Cost

The designer is a co-founder — zero content production cost beyond tools (~€10/mo). Characters are created as layered illustrations, rigged once in Live2D for unlimited animated clips. Content mix: ~70% static art (poster art, flash sheets, character cards), ~20% Live2D animated clips (5–15 sec), ~10% behind-the-scenes and venue content.

ToolsLive2D Cubism Indie (~€10/mo) · DaVinci Resolve (free) · Canva free tier
ProcessDesigner draws layered character art → rig once in Live2D (few hours/character) → animate unlimited clips from same rig
QualityPuppet-style motion suits the mid-century print aesthetic. Same art, perfect consistency across every post. No AI style drift.
Stage 2 — Mini-Series (when revenue supports it)
"Against All Blandness"

Wild, absurdist, serialized — set in Bulgaria, about snacks. Start with 1–2 short episodes (60–90 sec), not a full season. The Live2D pipeline scales into longer content once character rigs exist.

8-Week Smoke Test Calendar

Wks 1–2: Brand launch teaser, seal reveal, Blaze intro.   Wks 3–5: Character reveals (1 per reveal day) across the full cast.   Wks 6–8: Heat tier reveals, first LTO tease, bar photo drops, waitlist close.
