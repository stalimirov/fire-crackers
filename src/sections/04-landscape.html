The pattern is clear: every major snack market has multiple spicy brands. Latin America has Takis, Barcel, Sabritas. Asia has Samyang, Koikeya, Weilong. The US alone has 50+ hot SKUs. Europe — and especially Eastern Europe — is the last major whitespace. Fire Crackers enters a proven global category in a region where nobody owns it yet.
