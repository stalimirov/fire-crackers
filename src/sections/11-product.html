LTO Strategy — limited drops from Stage 2 onward

Every seasonal or special release is a mission. Announced with an animated teaser. Online-first for two weeks (FOMO). Then bar distribution. Batch sells out. Mission debrief posted. Examples: "Hot Mamma Beans," "Midnight Barley," "Spring Sting," "Balkan Inferno" (local pride edition). 15% documented sales lift from LTO model in comparable brands.

Packaging format
  • — Kraft paper stand-up pouch, heat-sealed
  • — Red wax seal stamp (the maker's mark)
  • — Hand-rendered character illustration per SKU
  • — Heat tier stamp in retro drawn style
  • — Ingredient list BG + EN (EU regulation)
  • — "Snack Responsibly" + 18+ mark — front, visible (brand voice, not a legal requirement — plays on hot-sauce culture)
  • — "Made in Bulgaria" with origin indicator
Future SKU opportunity

Mala positioning — Sichuan peppercorn numbing + spicy. Fastest-growing heat profile in Asia (2025). A "Shipka's Mala" variant (Bulgarian Shipka + Sichuan peppercorn) would be a genuine product innovation with cultural storytelling built in.
